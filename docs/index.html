I am an astrophysicist studying the light-scattering properties of cosmic dust, with a focus on comets, asteroids, and interstellar clouds. My work combines computational modelling with polarimetric techniques to understand how tiny dust particles shape our observable universe.

Research Focus

★

Cometary Dust

Scattering behaviour of dust in comets and asteroids as they approach the Sun.

◎

Polarimetry

Polarisation properties of dust in dark clouds and star clusters.

●

Umov Effect

Correlation between polarisation maxima/minima and scattering parameters.

◇

Dust Aggregates

Umov effect studies for silicate, organic, and carbon aggregates.
