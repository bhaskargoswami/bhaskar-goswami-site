Research

Research Overview

I investigate the light-scattering properties of cosmic dust and their impact on the observable universe. By combining computational modelling with polarimetric techniques, I study how tiny dust particles in comets, asteroids, and interstellar clouds influence planetary system formation and the evolution of life on Earth. My work spans:

  • ★ Scattering behaviour of dust in comets and asteroids as they approach the Sun.
  • ◎ Polarisation properties of dust in dark clouds and star clusters.
  • ● Correlation between polarisation maxima/minima and scattering parameters.
  • ◇ Umov effect studies for silicate, organic, and carbon aggregates.
