About

Short Bio

I am a physicist with a deep curiosity about the fundamental laws of nature and the physics of the universe. My research focuses on the light-scattering properties of cosmic dust and their role in shaping the observable universe. I investigate how tiny dust particles, such as those found in comets, asteroids, and interstellar clouds, influence planetary systems and contribute to the evolution of life on Earth.
